Classification
  • Mason:
    Type I: minimally displaced fracture (< 2 mm)
    Type II: partial articular fracture with displacement > 2 mm or angulation
    • Complete loss of cortical contact of at least one fracture fragment with the rest of the radius is associated with a complex injury pattern and warrants further investigation
    Type III: complete articular fracture with severe comminution
    Type IV: radial head fracture with ulnohumeral dislocation
    • Mason classification
  • OTA: radius/ulna proximal segment (21)
    Type A: extraarticular
    A1: ulna
    A2: radius
    A3: both
    Type B: articular, either radius or ulna
    B1: ulna fractured
    B2: radius fractured
    B3: articular in one, extraarticular in the other
    Type C: articular, both radius and ulna
    C1: simple fracture of both
    C2: simple fracture of one, comminuted fracture of the other
    C3: comminuted fractures in both
