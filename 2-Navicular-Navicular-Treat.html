Treatment
  • Objective: to restore talonavicular joint congruity and medial column length
  • Nonoperative treatment: SLC, NWB for 4-6 weeks followed by gradual return to activities over 6 weeks

    Cortical avulsion:
  • Nonoperative: indicated for small or nondisplaced fragments
  • Operative: indications include large or displaced fragments
    • Fragments involving < 25% of the articular surface can be excised, large fragments should be fixed with lag screws

    Tuberosity:
  • Nonoperative: indicated for nondisplaced fractures
  • Operative: indications include displaced fractures
    • Excise small displaced fragments and reattach posterior tibial tendon or fix large displaced fragments with lag screw

    Body:
  • Nonoperative: indicated for nondisplaced fractures
  • Operative: indications include displaced fractures and > 2 mm of articular incongruity in the talonavicular joint
    • ORIF to achieve anatomic reduction of the talonavicular joint
    • Lag screws can be used through a dorsomedial approach
    • Naviculocuneiform joints are immobile and can serve as a scaffold for transarticular fixation if needed in the setting of comminution
    • Bridge plating may be necessary to maintain medial column length
    • Talonavicular fusion can be performed for severe (> 40%) articular comminution

    Stress fracture:
  • SLC NWB for 6-8 weeks
  • Nonunions can be treated with bone graft and lag screw fixation
  • Consider early fixation with bone graft for complete and comminuted stress fractures

Complications
  • Osteonecrosis: risk increased with displacement and comminution or inadequate immobilization
    • May result in collapse requiring bone grafting and fixation to restore medial column length
  • Talonavicular posttraumatic arthritis: a consequence of any articular injury, can be theoretically minimized with anatomic reduction
    • May require salvage arthrodesis but due to the coupled motion of the hindfoot joints, this essentially eliminates motion at the subtalar and calcaneocuboid joints
