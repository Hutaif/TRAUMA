Classification
  • Mechanism
    Medial stress:
    • Inversion with adduction of midfoot on hindfoot
    • Fleck fractures of the dorsal talus, navicular, calcaneus or cuboid may indicate ligamentous instability
    Longitudinal stress:
    • Axial load transmitted through digits compressing midfoot against talus in foot plantarflexion
    • Typically results in navicular fractures
    Lateral stress:
    • "Nutcracker" injury: cuboid fracture due to compression between calcaneus and 4th/5th metatarsals
    • Most commonly associated with navicular avulsion fracture
    Plantar stress:
    • Plantar directed force causes avulsion fractures of the dorsal navicular, talus or anterior process of calcaneus
