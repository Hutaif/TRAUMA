Classification
  • Descriptive:
    Open or closed
    Location: proximal, middle, distal third
    Pattern: spiral, oblique, transverse
    Angulation: varus, valgus, rotational
    Displacement: shortening, translation
    Comminution, segmental or butterfly fragment

  • Winquist and Hansen: based on comminution - used for consideration of interlocking nails
    Type I: minimal or no comminution
    Type II: corticies of both fragments at least 50% intact
    Type III: 50-100% cortical comminution
    Type IV: circumferential comminution with no cortical contact at the fracture site

  • OTA: femur, diaphyseal (32)
    Type A: simple
    A1: spiral
    A2: oblique
    A3: transverse
    Type B: wedge
    B1: spiral
    B2: bending
    B3: fragmented
    Type C: complex
    C1: spiral
    C2: segmental
    C3: irregular
