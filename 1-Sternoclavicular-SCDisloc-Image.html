Imaging
    Radiographs
  • AP chest: identify asymmetry of the clavicles
  • Hobbs view: seated patient leans forward over plate and beam is shot from behind the neck
  • Serendipity view: supine patient, 40° cephalic tilt
    • Allows visualization of medial clavicle displacement

  • CT: best modality for SC joint evaluation
  • Arteriogram: evaluation of suspected vascular injury
