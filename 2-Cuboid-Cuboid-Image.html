Imaging
    Radiographs
  • AP, oblique and lateral views of the foot are standard examinations
    • Normal AP foot: yellow dotted line highlights normal TMT joint alignment
    Normal oblique foot: yellow dotted line hightlights normal TMT joint alignment
    Normal lateral foot
  • Multiple oblique views may be necessary to visualize cuboid joints
  • Weight-bearing or stress views should be obtained to assess ligamentous stability

  • CT: useful for identifying instability and extent of injury and should be considered for any fracture identified on radiograph
  • MRI: can be useful in diagnosing stress fractures
