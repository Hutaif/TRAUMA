Classification
  • Descriptive: based on displacement of proximal tibia relative to distal femur
    Anterior (30-40%): due to forceful hyperextension beyond 30°
    • Increasing risk to popliteal artery with increasing extension
    • PCL and possibly ACL injury
    Posterior (30-40%): due to posterior force on flexed knee
    • ACL and PCL injury
    • Increasing risk to popliteal artery with increasing displacement
    Lateral (rare): due to valgus force
    • MCL, ACL and PCL disruption
    Medial (rare): lateral and posterolateral structures disrupted
    Rotational (rare): due to varus/valgus with rotational component
    • Results in buttonholing of femoral condyle through capsule

  • OTA: tibiofemoral (40-A)
    1. Anterior
    2. Posterior
    3. Medial
    4. Lateral
    5. Other
